Brownies

Brownies are a type of chocolate-flavored baked dessert, typically made in square or rectangular shapes. They are known for their rich chocolate flavor and dense texture, often described as fudgy or cakey depending on the recipe and baking time.

Cupcakes

Cupcakes are small, individual cakes, typically baked in a paper or aluminum cup, and often frosted and decorated. They are a popular treat for various occasions and are known for their portability and portion control.

Cakes

Cakes are sweet, baked goods typically made with flour, sugar, eggs, and fat. They come in various forms, from large, layered cakes to smaller, individual portions.

Cookies

A "cookie" can refer to two distinct things: a small, sweet baked good, and a small file stored in a web browser. In the culinary sense, a cookie is a flat, sweet, baked treat, often made with flour, sugar, and butter

Bagels

A bagel is a bread roll originating in the Jewish communities of Poland. Bagels are traditionally made from yeasted wheat dough that is shaped by hand into a torus or ring, briefly boiled in water, and then baked.

Pastries

Pastry refers to a variety of doughs, as well as the sweet and savoury baked goods made from them. The dough may be accordingly called pastry dough for clarity. Sweetened pastries are often described as bakers' confectionery.

Pie

A pie is a baked dish typically characterized by a pastry crust enclosing a filling of sweet or savory ingredients. The crust can be a single layer, a bottom layer, or both top and bottom layers, with the filling placed inside. Common fillings include fruit, custard, or savory options like meat and vegetables.

Donuts

Donuts are sweet, fried pastries that are often ring-shaped or filled with various fillings. They are a popular breakfast item and come in many flavors and styles.
