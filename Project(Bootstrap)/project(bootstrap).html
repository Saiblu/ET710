Brownies

Brownies are a type of chocolate-flavored baked dessert, typically made in square or rectangular shapes. They are known for their rich chocolate flavor and dense texture, often described as fudgy or cakey depending on the recipe and baking time.

Cupcakes

Cupcakes are small, individual cakes, typically baked in a paper or aluminum cup, and often frosted and decorated. They are a popular treat for various occasions and are known for their portability and portion control.

Cakes

Cakes are sweet, baked goods typically made with flour, sugar, eggs, and fat. They come in various forms, from large, layered cakes to smaller, individual portions.

Cookies

A "cookie" can refer to two distinct things: a small, sweet baked good, and a small file stored in a web browser. In the culinary sense, a cookie is a flat, sweet, baked treat, often made with flour, sugar, and butter
